About Us

JobLynk is a job board website created by Group 7. Designed to bridge the gap between job seekers and employers, JobLynk offers a simple and effective way for users to find, apply for, and post job opportunities across various fields.

Our platform was built with real-world functionality in mind – aiming to help users quickly connect to opportunities that match their skills, goals, and career paths.

What We Offer:

  • A user-friendly interface for browsing and applying to jobs.
  • A platform for emloyers to post openings and find qualitief candidates.
  • Fast, organized access to opportunities – from internships to full-time roles.
  • Easy filtering by role, location, and category.

The project reflects our shared interest in technology, teamwork, and solving real problems through digital solutions. We hope JobLynk provides a glimpse of what's possible when innovation meets purpose.
